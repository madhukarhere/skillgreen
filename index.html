About US

SkillGreen global is a for-profit Project Management and Consultancy Company with the aims to bridge the critical gap in Rural Skill and Vocational Education & Training ecosystem by providing its services to Multilateral & Bilateral Donor Agencies, Institutional Donors, Impact Investors, Government Agencies & Corporates and any other agency working on Rural Skills.

Our Mission

SkillGreen Global’s mission is to increase outreach and effectiveness of skill building institutions that provide skills, extension & business development support in rural trades to significantly enhance livelihoods.

our services

SkillGreen takes a more holistic view towards training value chain by adopting – skill+ approach; and multi-stakeholder convergence framework. Services towards skill development and income enhancement are offered through market led, self-employment focused courses marked by standard course curricula & content, innovative training delivery methodologies and collective action. SkillGreen also offers various other ‘institutional development’ and ‘learning development’ support services that enables a partnering organisation’s offer relevant, effective skill development services viably and sustainably.
